The Artist

About

Dr Lekta is a UK underground hip-hop artist pushing a modern take on classic boom bap. Hailing from outside the traditional London spotlight, he brings a distinct voice to the scene — combining intricate lyricism, sharp wordplay, and gritty storytelling over sample-driven production.

Influenced by the golden era of hip-hop, Dr Lekta's sound channels the raw authenticity of 90s rap while maintaining a contemporary edge. His catalog showcases both technical ability and depth, with themes ranging from personal struggles to cultural commentary. Having collaborated with respected figures in hip-hop, Dr Lekta represents a lane that values substance over trends.
