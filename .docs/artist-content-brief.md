---

# 🎯 1. The Core Goal (Don’t skip this)

Most artists get this wrong:
A homepage is NOT just “info” — it should push ONE main action:

👉 **“Listen now” or “Follow on Spotify”**

Everything else supports that.

---

# 🧱 2. Must-Have Sections (What top artists include)

## 🔥 Hero Section (above the fold)

This is the most important part.

Include:

* Artist name + strong visual (professional photo or cover art)
* Short tagline (vibe-based, not generic)

  * e.g. *“Dark melodic trap from Spain”*
* Embedded Spotify player (latest release)
* ONE clear button:

  * “Listen on Spotify”

👉 Look at artists like Travis Scott or Central Cee — they immediately push you to music, not biography.

---

## 🎧 Latest Release / Music Section

* Highlight **1–3 strongest tracks only**
* Use:

  * Spotify embed
  * YouTube video (if available)

💡 Don’t overload — fewer songs = more plays per track.

---

## 📱 Social Proof Section

People trust what others already like.

Include:

* Monthly listeners (from Spotify)
* Playlist placements (if any)
* Collabs
* Quotes or press (even small blogs)

---

## 🎥 Visual Content

Hip-hop is VERY visual.

Add:

* Music videos
* Short clips (reels style)
* Performance footage

Artists like Playboi Carti and Bad Bunny rely heavily on aesthetic + visuals.

---

## 🧑‍🎤 About (Keep it SHORT)

Nobody reads long bios.

Good:

* 3–5 lines max
* Focus on identity + sound

Bad:

* Long life story

---

## 🔗 Links Section (Very Important)

Make it EASY to follow:

* Spotify
* Instagram
* TikTok
* YouTube

💡 Many artists use tools like Linktree, but your homepage can replace that if done well.

---

# 🚀 3. Growth-Focused Features (What actually boosts streams)

## ✅ Email / Fan Capture (Underrated)

Add:

* “Join the list” or “Get unreleased tracks”

Why?
👉 Social media can die — email doesn’t.

---

## ✅ Pixel Tracking (Advanced but powerful)

* Meta Pixel (Instagram ads)
* Google Analytics

This lets you:
👉 Retarget visitors later with ads

---

## ✅ Smart Links (Very important)

Use tools like:

* ToneDen
* Feature.fm

They:

* Route users to Spotify/Apple Music
* Track clicks
* Boost algorithm signals

---

# 🎨 4. Design Style That Works for Hip-Hop

Current trends:

* Dark mode / minimal
* Strong typography
* Gritty textures or clean luxury (depending on vibe)

Avoid:
❌ Generic website templates
❌ Too many colors
❌ Clutter

---

# ⚠️ 5. Common Mistakes (Most artists fail here)

* Too much text
* No clear CTA
* Too many songs at once
* No mobile optimization (THIS IS HUGE)
* No brand identity

👉 80–90% of traffic will be mobile.

---

# 🧠 6. What Successful Artists Actually Do

They:

* Drop a song → drive ALL traffic to ONE page
* Use Instagram/TikTok → funnel to Spotify
* Keep branding consistent everywhere
* Focus on **one strong release at a time**

---

# 🛠️ Simple Structure (You can copy this)

```
[Hero]
Name + image + Spotify embed + CTA

[Latest Release]
1–3 tracks

[Video]
Main music video

[Social Proof]
Stats / playlists

[About]
Short bio

[Links]
All platforms
```
